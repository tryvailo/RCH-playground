# **## About Us**  
#   
# **Treetops Court is set in pretty landscaped gardens in the market town of Leek. The home is convenient for shops, amenities and public transport.**  
#   
# **This purpose built care home provides nursing and social care for older people who are physically frail and older people who have mental health needs such as Alzheimer's disease. Accommodation at Treetops Court is provided in single occupancy rooms, the majority of which are en-suite. Some rooms are spacious enough for companions to share.**  
#   
# **Treetops Court has several lounges in which to relax and separate dining rooms, where residents can enjoy the company of others at meal times. Traditional home cooking is served using fresh produce. Daily choices are available and special diets can be catered for.**  
#   
# **Regular visits are made to the home by an experienced hair stylist and a qualified Chiropodist.**  
#   
# **Residents benefit from a varied programme of activities.**
