Frequently asked questions about Ladydale Care Home

What rating has Ladydale Care Home been given by the regulator?
Ladydale Care Home was given an overall rating of "Good" by the CQC in July 2018.
What types of care does Ladydale Care Home provide?
Ladydale Care Home provides Residential and Respite care.
How many rooms does Ladydale Care Home have?
Ladydale Care Home has 54 rooms.
Who owns Ladydale Care Home?
Ladydale Care Home is owned by Pearlcare.
Does Ladydale Care Home provide Residential care?
Yes. Ladydale Care Home does offer Residential care.
Does Ladydale Care Home offer Respite care?
Yes. Ladydale Care Home does offer Respite care.
Does Ladydale Care Home provide care for people with Dementia?
No. Ladydale Care Home does not provide care for people with Dementia.
What Local Authority does Ladydale Care Home come under?
Ladydale Care Home comes under Staffordshire.
