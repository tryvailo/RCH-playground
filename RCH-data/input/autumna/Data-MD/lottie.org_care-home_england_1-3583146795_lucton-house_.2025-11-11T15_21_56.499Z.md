## Overview

Lucton House, operated by [Gold Care Homes](https://lottie.org/care-home/operator/gold-care-homes/), is situated in [Birmingham](https://lottie.org/care-home/location/england/west-midlands/birmingham/) and offers a vibrant community life surrounded by lush gardens. This residential care home provides personalised care for up to 51 residents in modern, self-contained apartments. Lucton House excels in offering a variety of engaging activities, from arts and crafts to wine evenings, ensuring a fulfilling lifestyle. Residents can enjoy unique amenities such as a bar, library, hair salon, and cinema. The home promotes an active and healthy lifestyle, with a dedicated activities coordinator organising both indoor and outdoor experiences. Lucton House blends comfort, care, and community.

## Frequently Asked Questions

Below are some of the most commonly asked questions about Lucton House.

Where is Lucton House located?

It is situated in Birmingham and is close to popular amenities.

What is the CQC rating of Lucton House?

The [Care Quality Commission](https://lottie.org/care-guides/what-is-the-care-quality-commission/) (CQC) is England's health and social care services regulator. Lucton House is rated as **good**, based on the level of care and safety at the home.

What types of elderly care are available at Lucton House?

The following types of elderly care are available at this home: residential, dementia, respite.

How many beds does Lucton House have?

Currently, there are 51 beds at the home.

Who owns Lucton House?

This care home is owned and operated by Gold Care Homes.

What types of activities and events are available at this care home?

There are a variety of health and wellness activities available at Lucton House, including: Baking & Cooking, Beer, Cocktails & Wine, Visits from Children.

What is the cost of care at Lucton House?

Prices will vary from home to home, depending on the level of elderly care required. At Lucton House the cost of care starts from £1,100 per week.

It's best to call the home directly on 01527 917075 to find out about the total cost of care for your unique needs.
