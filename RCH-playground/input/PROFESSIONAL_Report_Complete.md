**1. CQC Ratings**

- Historical trends (3-5 years)
- Action plans for improvements
- Inspection dates

**2. FSA FHRS (Food Standards Agency)**
- Overall rating: 5/4/3/2/1/0
- 3 sub-scores: Hygiene, Cleanliness, Management
- Date of last inspection
- Specific compliance notes
- Special dietary considerations

**3. Companies House**
- Accounts filed for past 3 years
- Financial metrics:
  - Revenue
  - Profit/loss
  - Working capital
  - Assets & liabilities
  - Directors' salaries
- Filing timeliness (red flag if late)

**4. NHS Directory**
- Regulated status
- Inspection history
- Quality metrics
- Staffing ratios

**5. Google Places**
- Rating: 1-5 stars
- Review count & trend
- Review sentiment analysis (using ML)
- Photo gallery
- Opening hours & contact
- Visitor patterns (footfall analytics)

**6. Glassdoor**
- Employee satisfaction rating (1-5)
- Salary reviews
- Work-life balance score
- Management score
- Staff comments & sentiment
- Turnover insights

**7. LinkedIn**
- Staff profile data
- Average tenure at home
- Career progression patterns
- Qualification levels
- Department organization
- Hiring patterns

**8. Job Boards**
- Active job listings
- Job title & salary range
- Turnover signals
- Hiring frequency
- Department needs

### Proprietary Sources

**9. Autumna**
- Pricing data (current & historical)
- Price changes & trends
- Hidden fees identified
- Care package options

**10. Perplexity AI**
- Deep research on home
- Media mentions
- Incident reports
- Success stories
- Regulatory actions
- Owner/operator background

### Derived Intelligence

**11. Medical Capability Matrix**
- Staff qualifications against resident needs
- Specialist capabilities (dementia, diabetes, mobility)
- Medical equipment availability

**12. Financial Stability Score**
- Companies House data analysis
- Altman Z-score (bankruptcy risk)

- Glassdoor + LinkedIn data fusion
- Tenure stability score

**14. Visitor Engagement Score**
- Google Places review frequency
- Positive/negative sentiment %
