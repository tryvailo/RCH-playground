What to Ask During Your Visit

Use this checklist to ensure you gather all the information you need when visiting care homes.

Staffing: What is the staff-to-resident ratio? Are staff qualified and trained?
Activities: What activities and social programs are available? How often?
Meals: Can you see a sample menu? Are dietary requirements accommodated?
Medical Care: How is medication managed? Is there 24/7 nursing care if needed?
Visiting: What are the visiting hours? Can family visit anytime?
Pricing: What is included in the weekly fee? Are there additional charges?
Contracts: What is the notice period? Are there any hidden fees?
Facilities: What facilities are available? Garden, activities room, etc.?
Care Plans: How are care plans created and reviewed? Can family be involved?
Complaints: What is the complaints procedure? How are concerns addressed?
